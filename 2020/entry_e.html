PWS Cup 2020 Entry Instructions [WIP]

[Japanese]

[Participation Rules]

  • The contest is held as a team competition.
  • There is no limit to the number of members in a team
  • A team can have members from different organizations.
  • A team may participate in the contest without disclosing their organizations or personal names.
  • A team may choose to only participate in a preliminary round or final round.
  • Team members must not overlap between teams.
  • Each team must have a representative; the representative does not have to be a team member.
    • Students cannot be in charge. Please ask your lab teacher for help, etc.
    • The same person can become representatives of multiple teams.
  • There is no restriction on the software or hardware used in the contest.
  • At least one member from a team must register to CSS. The member must make a presentation at the PWS Cup session and later submit their presentation materials.
    • If this is difficult for some reason, please contact the PWS Cup organizing committee (see below for contact information).

[Notes]

  • The schedule is subject to change without notice due to system preparation and maintenance.
  • The final ranking will be decided by combining the results of both preliminary and final rounds.
  • We will inform the details of the contest rules to the teams before starting the preliminary round.
  • The data submitted in the contest will be disclosed after the contest.
  • Please read PWS Cup 2020 privacy policy (Japanese) regarding how we handle personal information.

[How to Register]

    • We will use the information you provide to send you the information you need to participate in the contest at a later date
